Csaba Hoch Links in post pages to syntax definition [post syntax, prop, reviewed] <hh/346> (2009-05-29) Children: hh/353 
When people read post pages, they will wonder sometimes what a
specific bracket segment does. I propose having a link to the
corresponding help section for each bracket segment.

Example post:

    Items:[-]
    - 1
    - 2

    [note This is ugly.]

Post page:

    <div>
    Items:<a href="somewhere/post_format.html#itemization">[-]</a>
    <ul>1</li>
    <ul>2</li>
    </div>

    <div>
    <red>[<a href="somewhere/post_format.html#note">note This is ugly.]</red>
    </div>

"somewhere" could be either http://heapkeeper.org or a custom
location.

This would make learning post syntax much easier for users.
