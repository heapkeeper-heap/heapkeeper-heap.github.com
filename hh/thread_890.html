Roadmap for v0.4 and v0.5

Csaba Hoch Roadmap for v0.4 and v0.5 [prop, reviewed, v0.4, v0.5] <hh/890> (2009-11-08) Children: hh/984 
I propose the following roadmap (which will not be set in stone of
course).

- New Generator:
    - Reviewing/understanding the new Generator //Attis
    - Discussing the new Generator //Attis & Csabi
    - Extending or modifying during and after the discussion
      //Attis & Csabi
    - Remove the old Generator //Csabi (or Attis)
- Releasing Hk v0.4 //Csabi
- Implementing support for post references (required for separating
  UMS and HH) [1] //Csabi
- Going through the heap and correct all post references so that Hk
  understands them
    - Hk-related posts (with or without "hh" tag): Csabi
    - other posts: Attis
- Implementing support for multiple heaps [2] //Csabi
- Separating UMS and HH with an automated process and check the result
  carefully //Attis & Csabi
- Making HH public:
    - setting up a public mailing list for HH //Attis or Csabi
    - setting up a github repo for HH //Attis or Csabi
    - setting up Shrek to make automatic commits of new posts //Attis
- Releasing Hk v0.5 and announcing Hk to the world //Csabi

We can implement smaller things on the way, of course, and during the
cleanup we can do more then just correcting the post references. I
want to refresh my memory and sort the Hk-related posts (we will see
how that goes).

You can work even on something bigger (hkweb for instance). Also, we
may do things paralel and may have branches in the repository: maybe
we will implement support for post references while still having
discussions about the new Generator.

Do you have ideas about what do you want to work on?

[2] heap://hh/536
