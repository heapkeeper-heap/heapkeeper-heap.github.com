The Erlang/OTP development moved party to GitHub. They still use
ClearCase internally, but they maintain a GitHub repository. Their
workflow [1] may be worth reading. They have a "pu" ("proposed
updates") branch that is rebased all the time, and which merges all
topic branches.

[1] http://wiki.github.com/erlang/otp/branches
